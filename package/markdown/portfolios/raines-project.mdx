## Project Overview

Raines Resources is a cutting-edge distance learning provider with over 45+ years of industry experience. The platform delivers proactive training systems across more than 30 different learning disciplines, including:

- High Risk Work
- Mining and Construction Machinery
- Basic Machine/Hand Tool Operations

Their unique, innovative approach to training ensures flexibility, safety, and comprehensive learning for individuals and businesses alike.

## Features

- Hands-on training modules  
- 30+ learning categories  
- Business and individual learning tracks  
- Seamless online course registration  
- User-friendly interface with powerful course discovery  
- Shopping cart and business onboarding system

## Technology Stack

- React / Next.js  
- Tailwind CSS  
- Server-side rendering for SEO  
- Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
